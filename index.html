How we are starting

1

Consultation

We start by getting to know you and your business: understanding your pain points, business goals, and any existing advertising campaigns. Once we have a clear idea of where you are currently at, we move onto the next stage - an audit

2

Audit

At this point, we go through your ad campaigns with a fine-tooth comb. This process takes up to three days. If your campaigns are well-organized, we will let you know and not propose our services. However, if there is room for improvement, we proceed to the next step: making an offer

3

Offer

After gathering all the relevant data and conducting the audit, we will present an offer. This offer will include details of our audit findings, possible outcomes from any changes implemented based on the audit, our strategies and methods for running advertising campaigns as well as a media plan if it is feasible to forecast potential leads or sales. If you agree with this proposal, we can start working together.
